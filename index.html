About us

Customer Complaint is important information reflecting customers sound and is a primary measure of customer dissatisfaction. An Effective and Efficient response to these complaints is an essential index of organizations performance. Complaints regarding electronic devices take a long time to be processed and are cumbersome for the users since service centers are not available everywhere. Manually processing of complaints involve a lot of inconvenience. Moreover the process of complaint redressal is not efficient and quick.

Online Complaint Management System aims to address the complaints of different users regarding various electronic devices in such a way that it minimizes customer dissatisfaction. The project aims to reduce the prolong processing of the complaints by categorizing the complaints on the basis of different devices and assigning different technicians for addressing the same. The proposed model aims to create a consumer grievance and support system, which accepts user complaints as a form and sorts them into different categories and priority. It maintains the record of the complaints lodged by the user and also displays the status of the processing of their complaints. The system aims at simplifying the process and providing efficient user experience.
